About Gershon Consulting

We are the bridge between France and the United States — a consulting firm staffed by Americans living in France who speak the language of both markets, literally and culturally.

Our Services →Partner Program →
🎯

Our Mission

Gershon Consulting was founded on a simple observation: French companies lose years and millions of euros trying to enter the US market without the right cultural and strategic guidance.

Meanwhile, thousands of highly qualified Americans live in France — people who know both cultures intimately, speak French fluently, and understand the business environments on both sides of the Atlantic.

We connect these two realities: building a practice that helps French companies succeed in America, powered by a network of American experts who call France home.

Our US Market Entry Framework

1

Market Assessment

Rigorous evaluation of US market opportunity: size, competitive landscape, buyer profiles, pricing benchmarks, and success probability. We tell the truth — including when the timing isn't right.

2

Foundation Building

Legal entity (Delaware LLC/C-Corp), US bank account, EIN, US address, insurance, and compliance groundwork. Getting the infrastructure right before spending on sales.

3

Market Positioning

Translating French value propositions into US market language. Competitive positioning, pricing for US buyers, reference story development, and US-adapted messaging.

4

Go-to-Market Execution

Sales process design, channel selection, partner recruitment, event strategy, and the first 90 days of active US sales — with an American consultant by the client's side.

5

Scaling & Optimization

First US hire, CRM setup, US PR strategy, Series A/B fundraising preparation, and transition to a standalone US operation.
