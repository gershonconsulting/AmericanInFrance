Phase 1 — US Market Assessment

Duration: 4-6 weeks  |  Deliverable: 30-page Market Assessment Report + Recommendation

Before spending a euro on US market entry, you need an honest assessment of your opportunity. We conduct rigorous research across six dimensions:

  • Market Size & Segmentation: TAM, SAM, and SOM analysis for your specific solution
  • Competitive Landscape: Direct and indirect competitors, pricing analysis, positioning gaps
  • Buyer Profile Research: Who actually buys this in the US? What do they care about? What triggers their purchase?
  • Channel Assessment: Direct sales, distribution, partnership, or marketplace — which fits your company?
  • Regulatory & Compliance Scan: FDA, FCC, state regulations, insurance — what do you need?
  • Success Probability Assessment: Honest evaluation of your company's readiness and the opportunity's attractiveness
💡

We Tell the Truth

Some companies aren't ready for the US market. Some products don't have US demand. We will tell you this — saving you years and millions — rather than take your money for a project destined to fail.

🏗️

Phase 2 — US Foundation Setup

Duration: 4-8 weeks  |  Deliverable: Fully operational US legal entity and infrastructure

  • US entity formation (Delaware LLC or C-Corp) — with recommendations on structure for your situation
  • EIN (Employer Identification Number) application
  • US business bank account opening (increasingly difficult for foreign-owned companies — we know the right banks)
  • US registered agent and business address
  • US business insurance (general liability, E&O, product liability as needed)
  • Trademark registration in the US (USPTO)
  • US website setup, hosting, and compliance (CCPA, ADA)
  • Initial payroll setup for first US hire
🎯

Phase 3 — US Market Positioning

Duration: 3-4 weeks  |  Deliverable: Complete US messaging platform and sales toolkit

  • Value proposition reframing for US buyers (ROI-focused, outcome-oriented language)
  • US competitive positioning and differentiation narrative
  • Pricing strategy for US market (benchmarked against US competitors)
  • US-adapted pitch deck (one-pager, investor version, customer version)
  • Case study development with US buyer perspective
  • US sales email sequences and LinkedIn outreach templates
  • Website copy review and US localization recommendations
🚀

Investor Introductions & Fundraising Support

For French companies seeking US capital, Gershon Consulting provides:

  • Delaware C-Corp conversion (required for US VC investment)
  • Investor pitch coaching for US audiences
  • Warm introductions to Franco-American investors (Partech, Idinvest, BPI USA programs)
  • AngelList, Y Combinator, and accelerator application support
  • SAFE vs convertible note vs priced round guidance
  • Cap table structure review for US investment compatibility
