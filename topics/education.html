Education in France for Americans & Their Families

From American bilingual schools in Paris to the French public system, Grande Écoles to US-accredited universities — here is the full education landscape for American families and students in France.

Option 1 — French Public Schools (École Nationale)

The French public school system is free, high-quality, and your right as a legal resident. Children are assigned to their local school (secteur scolaire) based on address.

  • École maternelle (preschool): ages 3–5. Now compulsory from age 3.
  • École primaire: ages 6–11 (CP through CM2)
  • Collège: ages 11–15 (6ème through 3ème)
  • Lycée: ages 15–18 (2nde, Première, Terminale) — culminates in the Baccalauréat
  • All instruction is in French — immersion is fast but support is available for non-French-speaking children (UPE2A classes)
  • Register at your local mairie (town hall) with proof of address, vaccination record, and passport
  • The French Bac is recognized by US universities for admission
🌍

Option 2 — International & American Schools in France

🦅

The American School of Paris (ASP)

Saint-Cloud, near Paris. PreK–Grade 12. US curriculum + IB. Excellent for Americans. ~€25,000–€30,000/year. asparis.org →

🌐

International School of Paris (ISP)

16th arrondissement. IB World School. Ages 3–18. Strong English program. ~€20,000–€25,000/year. isparis.edu →

🏰

Marymount International School Paris

Neuilly-sur-Seine. Catholic school, ages 3–18. IB programme. Welcoming to American families. marymountparis.com →

🎓

École Active Bilingue (EAB)

Multiple Paris locations. Bilingual French-English from age 3. Popular with expat families wanting French immersion + English continuity.

🌟

Lycée Français (AEFE Network)

For families wanting full French curriculum with global mobility. The Lycée Louis-le-Grand and others prepare students for Classes Préparatoires and Grandes Écoles.

🗺️

Schools Outside Paris

International schools exist in Lyon, Bordeaux, Toulouse, Nice, and Marseille. Check AAS (Association of American Schools in France) for full directory.

🎓

Higher Education: Universities & Grandes Écoles

  • French public universities: Extremely affordable (approx. €170–€600/year tuition). Top institutions include Sorbonne, Sciences Po, Paris-Dauphine, École Polytechnique.
  • Sciences Po Paris: English-taught programs available. Excellent for political science, international relations, economics. Highly respected in the US.
  • INSEAD (Fontainebleau): World top-10 MBA program. 1-year format. Strong US alumni network. insead.edu →
  • HEC Paris: Europe's #1 business school. MiM and MBA programs. French and English instruction.
  • American University of Paris (AUP): US-accredited liberal arts college in central Paris. Fully in English. US financial aid eligible. aup.edu →
  • Campus France: Official portal for studying in France — scholarships, visa info, university applications. campusfrance.org →
💡

US Student Loans & French Education

US federal student loans can be used at the American University of Paris (AUP), which is a US-accredited institution. They cannot be used at French public universities. Private lenders sometimes fund studies at recognized international schools — check with your lender.
