The Franco-American Community — Your Network in France

You're not alone. Hundreds of thousands of Americans live in France, served by a rich ecosystem of organizations spanning business networking, social connection, civic engagement, and professional development.

Business & Professional Networks

🏛️

AmCham France — American Chamber of Commerce

The premier Franco-American business organization. Regular events, advocacy, CEO roundtables, and connections to French and US companies. Ideal for consultants and executives.

Key Org
🤝

French-American Foundation

Nonprofit promoting Franco-American relations through Young Leaders program, cultural events, and policy dialogue. Strong alumni network of French and American leaders.

Foundation
🌐

BFA — Business France Alumni Network

Network of professionals who've participated in Business France programs — useful for connecting with French companies interested in US expansion.

Network
🗽

FACC — French-American Chamber of Commerce (NY)

US-based counterpart to AmCham. If you have US-based clients or contacts, FACC membership builds strong Franco-American business credibility.

Network
🚀

French Tech

Government-backed startup ecosystem with chapters across France and major US cities. Essential for tech-focused Americans and those working with French startups.

Official
🌍

Expat Social & Community Organizations

🌸

WICE — The International Community in Paris

Founded in 1978, WICE is one of Paris's oldest and most beloved expat communities. Educational courses, cultural events, volunteering, social clubs. Open to all nationalities.

Community
📰

Message Paris — English-Speaking Parents

Organization for English-speaking parents raising children in France. Playgroups, school guidance, expat parenting community.

Families
🌐

InterNations Paris

Large global expat network with active Paris chapter. Regular networking events, activity groups, and expat newcomer meetups.

Social
🤝

Meetup — American & English-Speaking Groups

Search "Americans in Paris," "English speakers Paris," or by interest. Dozens of active groups across Paris and major French cities.

Social
🗳️

Civic & Political Organizations

🐴

Democrats Abroad France

The largest American political organization in France. Hosts election events, voter registration drives, and local advocacy. Active groups in Paris, Lyon, Bordeaux and more.
democratsabroad.org →

🐘

Republicans Overseas

The conservative counterpart for American Republicans living abroad. Absentee voting assistance and community events.
republicansoverseas.com →

🌐

American Citizens Abroad (ACA)

Non-partisan advocacy organization fighting for tax reform for overseas Americans. Leads the campaign for residency-based taxation. Essential for anyone active on expat tax issues.
americansabroad.org →

📜

Association of Americans Resident Overseas (AARO)

Paris-based advocacy group for American citizens abroad. Regular events, policy alerts, and a library at their headquarters near the US Embassy.
aaro.org →

🏫

Educational & Cultural Organizations

🎓

American University of Paris (AUP)

US-accredited liberal arts university in Paris. American faculty and student culture, plus a large alumni network of Americans-in-France connections.
aup.edu →

🖼️

American Library in Paris

The largest English-language lending library on the continent. Events, author talks, children's programs. A beloved institution for English-speaking expats.
americanlibraryinparis.org →

🎭

American Church in Paris

More than a church — a community center hosting expat events, job fairs, support groups. The largest English-speaking church in Europe.
acparis.org →

🏛️

Centre Franco-Américain de la Mobilité

Promotes academic and professional exchange between France and the US. Partners with Fulbright, Campus France, and US universities.

🏥

Healthcare & Support Organizations

🏥

American Hospital of Paris

English-speaking hospital in Neuilly-sur-Seine. American-trained doctors, US-compatible insurance accepted. The expat community's go-to for serious medical care.

Essential
🏥

Hertford British Hospital

English-speaking hospital in Paris 8th. An alternative to American Hospital for English-speaking care.

Healthcare

Online Communities for Americans in France

💬

Reddit — r/expats & r/france

Large communities with Americans-in-France discussions on taxes, visas, banking, cultural adjustment, and daily life. Real peer advice, warts and all.

📘

Facebook Groups

Search "Americans in Paris," "Americans in France," "US Expats France." Active groups with thousands of members sharing real-time advice.

💼

LinkedIn — French Tech, AmCham Groups

Professional networking with Franco-American business focus. Key groups: AmCham France Members, French Tech, US-France Business Network.

🎙️

Podcasts for Americans in France

"Join Us in France," "Expat Focus," "The American in Paris Podcast" — English-language shows covering expat life in France.
