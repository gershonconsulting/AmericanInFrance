US Digital Marketing for French Companies

US digital marketing operates by different rules than European marketing. American buyers research extensively online before contacting sales. If your digital presence doesn't reach them at the right moment, your French competitor (or an American one) will.

US SEO — Getting Found by American Buyers

American B2B buyers Google their problems before they know they need your solution. US SEO is not optional — it's the foundation of US inbound marketing.

  • US English copywriting: Google.com ranks for US English. Your content must be written by native US speakers, not translated from French
  • US-hosted domain: A .com domain hosted on US servers with a US IP address signals geographic relevance to Google
  • US keyword research: Americans search differently from British or French English speakers. Use tools like Ahrefs, SEMrush, or Google Keyword Planner for .com US data
  • E-E-A-T: Google's Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, and Trustworthiness signals are critical — especially for B2B. Include author bios, certifications, and US client references
  • US business listings: Claim your Google Business Profile, Yelp (for B2C), Clutch (for B2B services), and industry-specific directories
  • Backlinks from US publications: Links from US industry publications, Forbes, TechCrunch, or relevant trade press signal US authority to Google

LinkedIn — The US B2B Marketing Engine

✍️

Content Strategy

American LinkedIn content: educational, insight-driven, tells a story. "Our company was featured in…" is less effective than "The 3 things we learned from 10 failed US sales calls."

👤

Personal Branding

US buyers engage with people more than company pages. Your CEO, US sales lead, or American consultant should post regularly and build a following before launching product campaigns.

🎯

LinkedIn Ads (US Targeting)

LinkedIn's US targeting by job title, industry, company size, and seniority is unmatched for B2B. Budget: minimum $5K/month for meaningful US campaigns. Use Sponsored Content + Lead Gen Forms.

🔗

Sales Navigator

LinkedIn Sales Navigator is essential for US outbound. Build targeted lists by industry/role/geography, track prospects, and enable warm outreach at scale.

📝

US Content Marketing — Educate to Convert

US B2B buyers consume 10+ pieces of content before making a purchase decision. Your content library is your pre-sales team:

📄

Case Studies (US Format)

US case studies follow a strict format: challenge → solution → measurable results. Include specific numbers ("Reduced costs by 23%" not "Significant savings"). Always get customer quote.

📊

Industry Reports & Data

Original research is the highest-value content in the US market. A "State of [Industry] 2025" report generates links, press coverage, and qualified leads.

🎙️

Podcast Appearances

The US has thousands of industry podcasts hungry for expert guests. A 30-minute podcast appearance reaches targeted buyers for 3-5 years via archives. No cost, just time.

🎬

Video Content

YouTube is America's #2 search engine. Short demos, explainer videos, and customer testimonials on YouTube/LinkedIn outperform text for complex B2B solutions.

📧

Email Newsletter

An email list of US prospects and customers is a critical business asset. Use ConvertKit, HubSpot, or Mailchimp with US-compliant unsubscribe options.

🌐

Webinars

US buyers attend webinars to learn — not to be pitched. Host "educational" webinars on problems your buyers face, with your product as the logical solution at the end.

📣

US PR & Media Strategy

  • Tech & Business Press: TechCrunch, Forbes, Business Insider, Fast Company — require genuine news (funding rounds, major partnerships, significant product launches)
  • Industry Trade Press: Every US industry has trade publications. These are easier to get into than major outlets and reach more targeted buyers
  • HARO / Qwoted: Help a Reporter Out (now Connectively) connects journalists with expert sources. Responding to journalist queries builds credibility and backlinks for free
  • French Tech Network: Business France and La French Tech have PR programs for French companies in the US — leverage these official channels
  • Press Releases: US-style press releases are shorter and more direct than French ones. Lead with the news, not the company history
  • Hire a US PR Agency: For sustained US media presence, a US-based PR agency (or a bilingual Franco-American specialist) is typically worth the investment

US Paid Digital Advertising

🔍

Google Ads (US)

Competitive CPCs in US B2B markets: $5-50+ per click for business software keywords. Requires compelling US landing pages, clear ROI messaging, and proper US tracking setup.

💼

LinkedIn Ads

Most expensive (CPM $60-100+) but highest B2B targeting precision. Best for enterprise deals where one customer is worth $50K+.

📘

Meta (Facebook/Instagram)

More effective for B2C and SMB targeting than enterprise B2B. Can be cost-effective for top-of-funnel awareness with US buyer lookalike audiences.

🎯

Intent-Based Advertising

Tools like G2, Bombora, Rollworks, and 6Sense target US companies actively searching for your category. Higher intent = higher conversion rates than traditional display.
